Responsible Gambling

Bet Responsibly

At SportsBook67, we're committed to promoting responsible gambling practices. Sports betting should be entertaining and fun, never a source of financial stress or personal harm.

If you or someone you know is struggling with gambling addiction, help is available:

  • National Problem Gambling Helpline: Call 1-800-GAMBLER (1-800-426-2537) for free, confidential support 24/7
  • Self-Exclusion Programs: All licensed sportsbooks offer self-exclusion tools that allow you to restrict your access for a set period. Contact your state's gaming commission for more information.
  • Set Limits: Before you start betting, set clear limits on how much time and money you're willing to spend. Stick to these limits, and never chase losses.
  • Know the Signs: Be aware of warning signs like betting more than you can afford, lying about gambling, or feeling anxious when you can't bet.

For more resources and information, please visit our Responsible Gambling page.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

SportsBook67.com uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and collect analytics data. Here's what you need to know:

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, improve performance, and provide personalized experiences.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality, including authentication (remembering that you're logged in), session management, and security features. These cookies are necessary for the site to function and cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used by Google Analytics and Cloudflare to understand how visitors interact with our site. These cookies collect information about page views, time on site, bounce rates, and user flows. This data is anonymized and used to improve our content and user experience.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences, such as your state selection or display preferences, to provide a more personalized experience on return visits.

Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that may set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Tracks website usage and provides analytics reports. Google's privacy policy applies to their use of this data.
  • Cloudflare: Provides content delivery, security, and performance optimization. Cloudflare may set cookies for security and performance purposes.
  • Supabase: Our authentication and database provider may set cookies to manage your login sessions securely.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, though this may affect your ability to use certain features of our site. To learn more about managing cookies, visit your browser's help documentation.
